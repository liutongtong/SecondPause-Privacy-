Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 06, 2026

Overview

This app is designed to provide a calm space for reflection and gentle voice interaction. User privacy and dignity are treated with the utmost care.

Information We Collect

  • Voice input (microphone)
    The microphone is used only when the user actively chooses to speak.
    Voice input is sent to the Android system speech recognition service to convert speech into text.

What We Do Not Do

  • We do not record audio
  • We do not store voice data
  • We do not collect personal information
  • We do not share data with third parties
  • We do not track users
  • We do not use analytics or advertising services

How Voice Data Is Handled

Speech recognition is handled entirely by Android system services. The app receives only the recognized text result and does not retain it after use.

Purpose of Use

Voice input is used solely to:

  • Detect whether speech was present
  • Provide a gentle, calming spoken response

Health and Wellbeing Disclaimer

This app is not a medical, mental health, or emergency service.
It does not provide diagnosis, treatment, or crisis support.
If you feel unsafe or distressed, please contact family members, trusted friends, or local support services.

Children

This app is suitable for general audiences. It is not directed specifically at children.

Changes

This policy may be updated if app features change.
